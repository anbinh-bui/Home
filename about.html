Hi, I’m An-Binh Bui, I'm originally from Vietnam and currently a student at Ecole Polytechnique & HEC Paris Double Degree in Data Science & Quant Finance. Before France, I studied Economics and Computer Science at Bocconi University, Milan, Italy.

I love math, language, life-science, sustainability, and have a long, zig-zaggy journey through them all. Starting with math olympiad from grade 3, in my last year of middle school, solving problems, though fun, faded to my fascination with Chinese and Eastern medicine, which I believed are more practical. I then majored in Chinese at a national gifted highschool and nearly headed to Toronto for biochemical engineering after. Yet when the pandemic spread, when spending time trying all STEM-related courses, I decided that I’d love something techier yet more human. That led me to my data/computer science and finance path right now.

At and beyond Bocconi I picked up advanced math, econometrics, and machine learning and was fascinated with power of computing in life as it can model and drive my many fields of interest, from language, medicine, to philanthropic impact. That drove me to explore careers in quant and AI/ML in fintech.
